Title
Articles on Covid most read on Wikipedia in 2020
 
Link
https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/business/articles-on-covid-most-read-on-wikipedia-in-2020/articleshow/80166741.cms
 
Date
Jan 8, 2021, 17:49  IST
 
Text
In 2020, articles on Covid-19 pandemic were the most read on Wikipedia. The article on Covid was clicked about 84 million times, almost double of the number of click 2019's most read article about the Marvel movie Avengers: Endgame received. The second slot was taken by the article on Donald Trump with 57.1 million views.
