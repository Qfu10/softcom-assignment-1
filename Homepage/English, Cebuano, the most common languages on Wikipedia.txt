Online encyclopedia Wikipedia went online 20 years ago. Since then, the website has become an indispensable reference for the world despite being reliant solely on the work of volunteers for article research and editing. The busy bees behind the Wikipedia scenes are currently curating more than 55.6 million articles in more than 300 languages. English and Cebuano are the most common languages on Wikipedia.
